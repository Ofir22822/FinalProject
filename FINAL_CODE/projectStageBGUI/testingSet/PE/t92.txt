The first stop on your small group tour is the old market town of Vic and its beautiful cathedral.
Next youll visit the stunning stone-built village of Queralbs from where youll catch the rack railway high into the Pyrenees to Nuria.
Here you can spend the rest of the day exploring the beautiful mountain scenery following hiking routes horse riding or boating.

Please wear warm clothes and good walking shoes on this tour.
This tour does not require any special physical condition but a reasonable level of fitness is needed if you wish to walk in the Vall de la Coma de la Vaca.
Its up to you how far and how high you want to go!Please note From the 1st of November to the 30th of November due to annual track maintenance the rack railway will be closed Monday to Friday.
In the instances that the railway is closed we will find an alternative itinerary such as going up into the mountain valley by road or enjoying a mountain walk at lower levels.
These changes will in no way take away from your Pyrenees mountain experience.
