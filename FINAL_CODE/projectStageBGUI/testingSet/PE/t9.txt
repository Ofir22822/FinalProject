Museum Rothenburg Discover the TOP 100 at www.germany.travel/top100 Medieval Crime Museum Rothenburg The Medieval Crime Museum in Rothenburg comprises two buildings containing several hundred exhibits from all over the German-speaking world.
Display on the map Add to/remove from travel planner These artefacts illustrate how the judicial system developed from the High Middle Ages through to the 19th century.
Displays shed light on medieval trials instruments of torture and the tools used to inflict pain or to execute.
The civil policy orders reveal how even the most private spheres of peoples lives were controlled by regulations on clothing marriage and baptism.
