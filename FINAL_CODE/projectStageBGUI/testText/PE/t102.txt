Mankin-French/CRT Lorraine There is hardly time to dump your suitcases in Verdun before going to the national forest located at the gates of the city.
Created after the war on the old battlefield it had the difficult task of healing this wounded land.
Covering almost 10000 km2 25000 acres this forest is an authentic open-air museum with no less than 80 historical monuments from the Great War or built in memory of the combatants.

On the last floor you cannot miss the spectacular view of the forest.
