Choice 2017 Schloss Dankern Holiday Resort Discover the TOP 100 at www.germany.travel/top100 Visitor information Open early April to early November Events www.schloss-dankern.de Schloss Dankern holiday resort number one for leisure and recreation The baroque moated castle of Schloss Dankern offers a variety of leisure activities for all the family and is the centrepoint of this exceptional holiday resort in Haren at the heart of the scenic Emsland region.
Display on the map Add to/remove from travel planner No matter whether you spend just a day or a whole holiday at Schloss Dankern boredom doesnt stand a chance come rain or shine the 200-hectare site offers more than 200 leisure sports and kids activities for all-round family fun.
Children can play at being knights at the huge Drago Castle or have lots of fun on the giant water slide.
The covered Spielland play area 10000mÂ² with its new indoor activity centre features snake slides wave slides an ice rink bouncy castles climbing walls indoor bumper cars pool darts carousels soft ball cannons pony riding and slide towers â€“ perfect for young adventurers.
Water babies can splash around in the outdoor pool and tropical leisure pool or at Lake Dankern with its sandy beach.
Other facilities include a spinning tower family go-karting a high ropes course with exceptionally tricky sections a low ropes course for children over 1.25 metres mini golf badminton tennis table tennis bowling an inline skating course pedalos a childrens disco and a cinema.
Highlights Perfect swimming weather and water at just the right temperature are guaranteed at the TOPAS leisure pool.
The slides ranging from four to 110 metres are lots of fun for children.
Other features include submerged seating a jacuzzi a water cone fountain and a separate pool for the little ones with play equipment.
If you enjoy being on the beach be sure to head to Lake Dankern where you can relax and unwind on the white sand while the kids build sandcastles or have fun burying dad! For sporty visitors there is windsurfing a waterskiing circuit seabob rental and a diving school with equipment hire for taster sessions.
And if you want to spend your whole holiday here woodland lakeside and heathland holiday homes with modern furnishings and terraces are available.
They provide a cosy home-from-home for families looking to relax and recharge their batteries.
A carefree holiday wouldnt be complete without good food and there are plenty of restaurants here that cater for all the family.
