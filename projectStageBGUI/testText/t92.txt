Welcome to a winding 450 km tour through the majestic panorama of the Bavarian Alps from Lindau on Lake Constance to Berchtesgaden on Lake KÃ¶nigssee.

The stunning views and the sense that you need only jump to catch a glimpse of the other side of the Alpine ridge mean that you are truly on the German Alpine Road the banqueting hall of the Alps.
A chocolate box scene Over 20 crystal clear lakes proud castles fairytale palaces over 60 spa resorts locks valleys hundreds of peaks and sun-kissed Alpine pastures used by herdsmen to make delicious mountain cheeses can be found all along the route.
You can also enjoy gastronomic delights in rustic cottages cosy beer gardens or lakeside cafes and can try your hand at activities such as rafting watersports paragliding cycling hiking climbing or even wintersports at one of the regions numerous ski resorts.
It doesnt get better than this As you travel along the route you can either choose to spend your holiday on a farm or stay over in a luxurious lodging house.
