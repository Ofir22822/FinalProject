The replacement of Art Nouveau with Modernist art after the First World War saw the application of a new style marked by flat roofs clean line and well-lit interiors Functionalism.
This style where technical intentions and purpose go hand-in-hand with art found wide application in buildings of all types and sizes.
The most beautiful functionalist villas Which Functionalist buildings are the best? The ones that are simple and austere while being economical and comfortable in harmony with their surroundings and conform to the terrain.
A vivid example of all these hallmarks of the style can be seen at Villa Tugendhat in Brno designed by Ludwig Mies van der Rohe.
As one of the founding works of global modern architecture the Villa has been included on the list of UNESCO Heritage Sites.
