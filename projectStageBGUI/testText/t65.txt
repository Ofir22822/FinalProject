Focused on the subject of mobility it offers a variety of exciting attractions and activities.
Display on the map Add to/remove from travel planner Autostadt Wolfsburg takes you on a fascinating journey through the world of the automobile.Set in more than 28 hectares of parkland a mixture of stunning architecture design and nature provides the framework for a wide range of attractions.
Exhibitions feature classic cars through the ages and focus on what made them so groundbreaking at the time.
Works of art and short films offer new perspectives on automotive subjects while interactive research installations provide hands-on experiences.
