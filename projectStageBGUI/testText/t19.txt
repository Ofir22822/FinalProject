Here you can enjoy the sweetness of life drawn from the historic pedestrian center as you stroll through its streets admiring the buildings facades.

Sail on the Launch river which irrigates the canals crossing the district and begin reliving the journey of market gardeners who used to cross it to take their products to the market building.
A must-see spot is the fishmongers district connecting Little Venice to the area of Tanneurs.
It was here in these houses with colorful and sometimes quirky facades where the fishermen of the city lived.
The pier also served as an auction house.
